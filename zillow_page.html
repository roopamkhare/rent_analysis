    Board-on-board fence for privacyHand-scraped hardwood floorsStainless steel appliancesCustom granite countersWalk-in closetGas cooktopSpacious gourmet kitchen
    Outstanding 1-story home with luxury features such as hand-scraped hardwood floors, archways, and a see-through fireplace between the living room and dining room. The spacious gourmet kitchen is open to the family room and features custom granite counters, breakfast bar, island with overhead pot rack, stainless steel appliances, gas cooktop, and pantry. The oversized (19' x 13') master suite is separate from the other bedrooms for greater privacy. The master bath features a jacuzzi tub, separate shower, walk-in closet and 2 separate vanities. The second living area is outside the 2 other bedrooms making a perfect retreat. Would also make a great game room or media room. The backyard is great for entertaining - it features a board-on-board fence for privacy and a large flagstone patio with a water feature. This home is part of the Stonebridge master-planned community with numerous amenities including golf, tennis & pickleball courts, skate park, beach club, aquatic center, many parks & playgrounds, and over 20 miles of hike and bike trails. This home is only .25 miles from Eddins Elementary and Falcon Creek Park.
